Steak Recipe

Below are the ingredients you need to prepare a steak

  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 taablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup or tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 1/2 teaspooon finley minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Dash crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 top round steak

Once you ahve prepared these ingredients, we move on to the steps to prepare our delicious steak.

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Combine the vinegar, oil, sauce and every other thing
  3. put the steak in a large food bag.
  4. preheat the broiiler and remove the meat from marinade.
  5. broil for 6-9 minute depending on the meat size.

And there we go, you have prepared a delicioius steak to enjoy.
