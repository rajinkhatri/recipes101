Wings Recipe

Below are the ingredinets you need to make yourself some wings.

  • 2 1/2 pounds chicken wings(tips discarded)
  • 3/4 red hot buffalo wing sauce
  • blue cheese dressing
  • celery sticks

once all these ingredients are prepared, lets move on to cooking them.

  1. Deep fry wings at 450 degrees for 12 minutes or until cooked.
  2. toss cooked wings in a large bowl and dip in red hot sauce
  3. serve with celery sticks and blue cheese dressing

And there we go, the wings are ready to be served.
