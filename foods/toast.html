Toast Recipe

Below are the ingredients you need to prepare a toast

  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 4 slices bread

once you have prepared these ingredients, we move on to the steps to prepare the toast.

  1. Beat egg, vanilla, cinnamon in shallow dish with wire whisk. Stir in milk.
  2. Dip bread iin egg mixture, turning to coat both sides evenly.
  3. Cook bread slices on lightly greased griddle

Toast is extremely easy to prepare so enjoy.
