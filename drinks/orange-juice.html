Orange juice Recipe

Orange juice canbe the best start for your day and its ingredients are also very easily obtained.

  • 10 oranges

Now we prepare fresh orange juice.

  1. Wash the oranges to remove any potential pesticide residue.
  2. Cut the oranges in half.
  3. Using a hand held citrus reamer, twsit the flesh of the oranges to extract the juices.
  4. pour the freshly squeezed orange juice into a glass and enjoy.

Enjoy your orange juice.
