Pina colada Recipe

A classic pina colada is a sweet, tropical cocktail made with rum, pineapple juice anf coconut cream.

the ingredients for pina colada are

  • 2 ounces light rium
  • 2 ounces cream of coconut
  • 4 ounces pineapple juice
  • 1 cup crushed ice
  • pineapple wedge and maraschine to garnish

And the steps to prepare a pina colada for yourself:

  1. Place ice, coconut cream, rum and pineapple juice in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Serve in a hurricane glass tarnished with pineapple wedge, and umbrella.

Then enjoy your drink.
