Blue mocktail recipe

Mocktail goes great with any food and below are the ingredients to prepare the mocktail.

  • 1/2 cup blue curacao syrup
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoon orange juice
  • 2 cups lemon lime seltzer
  • 2 lemon slices
  • 2 orange slices

following are the steps to prepare a blue mocktail.

  1. Fill two highball glasses with ice.
  2. pour 1/4 of the blue curacao syrup into each glass.
  3. Add the lemon and orange juice to each glass.
  4. Top each mocktail with the seltzer and use a cocktail stirrer to give each glass a quick stir
  5. Garnish each glass with a lemon slice and an orange slice.

And there you have your fine mocktail.
